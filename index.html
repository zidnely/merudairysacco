Who We Are

Meru Dairy Savings and Credit Co-operative Limited is a financial cooperative located in Meru County.

We operate as a Sacco, with a primary focus on providing financial services and support to dairy farmers in the region.

Read More

Vision, Mission and Core Values

Vision

To be the most preferred Dairy Sacco in Kenya.

Mission

Transforming lives of dairy farmers by offering timely, effective, and innovative financial solutions.

Our Core Values

Integrity, Team Work, Fairness, Reliability, Innovative and Accountability.

Products

The Sacco offers a wide range of financial services in BOSA

Mkulima Development Loan

Build cow sheds, Buy feeds, Milking machines, Chuff cutters and Dairy cows.
Repayment period maximum 48 months
Four times the deposits.

Read More

Mkulima Development Top-Up Loans

The applicant must have serviced the development loan or at least 6 months,
25% of the development loan granted.

Read More

Shirika Development Loan

The loan is granted to dairy cooperatives
-Minimum share capital - Ksh 10,000
-Minimum repayment period 48 months

Read More

Shirika Advance Loan

-Minimum share capital - Ksh 10,000
-Repayment period 6 Months
-Maximum Loan amount 6 months

Read More

Mkulima Advance Loan

-Multiplier will be 3 times the deposit
-Repayment period is 3 months
-Maximum amount 50,000

Read More

Maziwa Manufaa/Production Loan

Constant income from dairy farming through the Meru Dairy Union for 3 months
-Repayment period 24 months
-Monthly share contribution of 2% and not less than Ksh 500
-Maximum loan amount is 1 Million Shillings
-Cows should be insured.

Read More

Elimu Loan

-The borrower to provide a fee structure from the school
-Repayment period 12 months
-3 times the deposits
-At least 3 guarantors

Read More

Livestock Insurance Premiums - Bima Loan

Amount depends on the livestock value.
Repayment period for Bima Loan is 10 months.

Read More

Staff Development Loan

Subject to the 2/3rd rule.
Repayment period is 60 Months for permanent staff and on the contract period for those on the contract.
Maximum amount - Ksh 3 Million.

Read More

Staff Emergency Loan

Advanced to Meru Dairy Staff on a short term basis.
Repayment period does not exceed 12 months.
Maximum amount - Ksh 50,000.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

The application process for the Mkulima Development Loan is simple. You can start by filling out an online application form on our website, providing the necessary details about your farming project and financial needs.

The interest rates for the Mkulima Advance Loan are competitive and will depend on various factors, including the loan amount and repayment terms. You can find detailed information about the interest rates on our official website.

The disbursement of the Shirika Development Loan is typically processed promptly upon approval. You can expect the funds to be transferred to your account within a short period, enabling you to initiate your development projects.

The Maziwa Manufaa/Production Loan is designed to support dairy farming activities. It provides financial assistance for the production and enhancement of milk-related projects, helping farmers boost their dairy output and overall productivity.

Yes, there are specific eligibility criteria for the Elimu Loan. To qualify, applicants typically need to provide details about their educational pursuits and demonstrate the purpose of the loan towards education-related expenses.

For inquiries about the Livestock Insurance Premiums - Bima Loan, you can contact our customer service team through the provided channels on our website. They will be happy to assist you with any questions or concerns regarding this specific loan.
